The brief

Aurora Studio had outgrown a slow, dated website that buried their best work. They needed a site that felt as considered as the work itself — fast, minimal, and built to convert curious visitors into real enquiries.

The challenge

The old site took too long to load, wasn't mobile-friendly, and gave search engines (and AI) almost nothing to understand. Bounce rates were high and enquiries were trickling in.

What we did

We rebuilt the site from the ground up: a clean, content-first design; lightning-fast performance; structured data and clear semantics so both people and AI can read it; and a simple enquiry flow that makes getting in touch effortless.
